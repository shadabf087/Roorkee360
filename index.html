About Roorkee360

Roorkee360 is a visual storytelling project dedicated to capturing the essence of Roorkee - its people, places, culture, and daily life. Through our Instagram account @roorkee08, we share moments that define this beautiful city.

Our mission is to showcase Roorkee's unique charm and hidden gems, from its historical architecture to modern developments, from natural beauty to urban life.

Follow our journey as we explore and document the many facets of this wonderful city, one photo at a time.
